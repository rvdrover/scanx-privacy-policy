Information Collection

ScanX does not collect any personal information such as name, email, phone number, or address. The app may access the following device data to provide its functionality:

  • Files & Documents: To save or export scanned QR code data or Images.
  • Device Information: Unique device identifiers to monitor app performance and for analytics.
  • WiFi Information: To connect to WiFi networks via QR codes (location permission may be required for WiFi scanning on Android).
  • Crash Logs & App Performance Data: Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics to improve app stability and functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AdMob: Provides advertisements within the app. All ads are non-personalized.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Collects anonymous usage data, crash logs, and performance metrics for analytics purposes. No personal information is collected.

Data Sharing

ScanX only shares data with third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or ads. No personal information is shared with any third party.

Audience

ScanX is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13/16.

User Control

Users can choose when to save or share files. Users may also opt-out of analytics collection by disabling data collection in the device settings or app preferences if provided.

External Links

ScanX does not contain any links to external websites or third-party services.
